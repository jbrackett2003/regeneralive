## 🏪 The "Networks" — Apply to These First

Affiliate networks are platforms that host hundreds of brands at once. Once you're approved on a network, applying to individual brands inside is much faster.

### 1. Impact (impact.com)
- **Why:** Hosts AG1, Oura, Bulletproof, Thrive Market, ButcherBox, Caraway, Our Place, Magic Spoon, Four Sigmatic, Ritual, etc.
- **Apply:** https://app.impact.com/campaign-promo-signup/Impact.brand *(create a publisher account)*

### 2. ShareASale (shareasale.com)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Don't apply with an empty site.** Make sure each category has at least 3-4 products and a few articles before applying. (You're already there ✓)

2. **Disclose properly.** All US affiliate links require FTC disclosure — your `/disclosure` page handles this ✓

3. **Pace yourself.** Apply to 3-5 programs per week. Each application takes 5-10 minutes if done right.

4. **Track your application status.** Create a Google Sheet:
   `Brand | Network | Date Applied | Status | Commission | Cookie | Notes`

5. **Re-apply if rejected.** After 60 days with more content, you can reapply to most networks.

6. **Focus on high-commission, low-friction first.** Athletic Greens (35%) and Eight Sleep ($250-500/sale) deserve special attention.

7. **Get a tax form ready.** Most US programs require a W-9. Have it filled out and PDF'd.

## 🎁 Bonus: Programs to Avoid

- **Amazon Associates** — only 1-3% commission, 24-hour cookie. Not worth it unless you have very high traffic.
- **Walmart Affiliate** — same issue, low commission.
- **Drop-shipping affiliate networks** (e.g. Wish, AliExpress) — bad fit for editorial integrity.
- **Anything that requires you to buy product to "qualify"** — that's MLM, not affiliate.
